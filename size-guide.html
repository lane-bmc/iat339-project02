Measuring Your Fit

To measure your clothing size, simply do the following:

  • CHEST
    Run a flexible tape measure across the fullest area of your chest, holding the tape measure horizontally.
  • WAIST
    Run a flexible tape measure around the narrowest part of your waist, keeping the tape horizontal.
  • HIP
    Stand with your feet together and measure around the fullest point of the hip, keeping the tape measure horizontal.

Picking The Right Size

So picking the right size is a pretty simple process. But what if you're stuck in between two sizes?

The choice really is yours. Do you like a tight fit? Go for the smaller size. Prefer a baggier cut? Go for the larger size. Do your chest and waist measurements result in two different suggested sizes? Order the size from your chest measurement.
