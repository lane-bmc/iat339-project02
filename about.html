About Us

Fit to a Tee is a Vancouver-based t-shirt company that makes limited edition, short run, one of a kind t-shirts. We design unique styles which are only available for one month, so that everything stays nice and fresh. We want you to look great in a t-shirt that few other people can call their own, and we want you to feel great about wearing it.

What We Believe In

Uniqueness.

Our styles are only run for 1 month, so you can be sure that you'll be one of a few lucky people to own your unique style.

Sustainability.

Our t-shirts are made in Canada from a 100% bamboo tri-blend, totally sweatshop free. That's right, they're environmentally and socially conscious.

Great Design.

Our shirts are designed by professional artists and graphic designers with energy and creativity to spare, who want to share our work. We love making t-shirts and being proud of what we wear.

Meet Our Team

Justin Schurtz

CEO and Founder

Justin is the driving force behind Fit To A Tee and the man who started it all. After graduating with a joint major in business and design from Simon Fraser University, he spent 6 years as a digital designer, working for companies such as Microsoft and SAP. He later decided that he wanted to start his own business, and founded Fit To A Tee in 2012. He continues to design styles every once in a while.

When he has free time, you can probably find him climbing trees or skiing up in Whistler, British Columbia.

Shirley Knott

Lead Designer

Shirley's the creative mastermind behind many of Fit To A Tee's unique styles, and she's also responsible for connecting with great local artists who want to share their work as well. After graduating from the Emily Carr University of Art + Design, she worked at Ion Brand Design for 4 years before coming to Fit To A Tee in 2013.

When she's not working on rad new designs, Shirley loves reading Keats, smelling flowers, and playing the piano.

Chris Anthemum

Prepress Lead

Chris has a passion for taking the digital and making it real. He's worked at various print shops across the Lower Mainland including Made In Print and True North Screen Printing, and has taken his talents to Fit To A Tee. He's the real deal.

Chris also plays drums with his band National Anthemum, and enjoys long walks on the beach in the cold Vancouver rain.
